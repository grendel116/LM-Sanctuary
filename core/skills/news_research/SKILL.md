# SKILL: Liberation & Working-Class Deep Research

Use this skill when researching labor news, indigenous struggles, strikes, union organizing, and global anti-capitalist actions to produce ground truth reports.

1. **Establish a Recursive Research Plan**:
   - Begin by defining the core questions, historical context, and material conditions of the struggle.
   - Generate initial search queries targeting specific union locals, indigenous campaigns, geographic locations, and corporate/state adversaries.

2. **Target Class-Conscious and Independent Outlets**:
   - Prioritize independent and working-class publications such as *Labor Notes*, *Labor Tribune*, *Peoples Dispatch*, *Real News Network*, *Indigenous Environmental Network*, *Red Media*, and *Labor Start*.
   - Gather a diverse set of **5 to 7 distinct, high-quality sources** to ensure breadth, verifying key events and statistics across multiple reports.

3. **Iteratively Search and Refine**:
   - Execute search queries to find articles, and use the `read_webpage` tool to extract clean Markdown text.
   - Analyze gathered articles, identify gaps in facts (such as strike sizes, demands, local names, dates, or outcomes), and generate targeted follow-up queries for the next search iteration.
   - Log your intermediate analysis and reasoning steps (thoughts) clearly.

4. **Synthesize a Grounded Materialist Report**:
   - Structure the final report around concrete material realities: economic targets, specific union/indigenous organizations, dates, locations, physical conditions, and demands.
   - List the 5 to 7 distinct sources clearly with their URLs at the end of the report as references.
   - Present the report in structured Markdown with clear headings.
